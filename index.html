03 How we work

Three phases. No slide decks.

  1. I

    Scope

    Diagnose the problem. Define success in numbers. Decide what not to build.

    Two weeks of research, prototyping and hard conversations. By the end, everyone — engineering, design, your CEO — is pointed at the same outcome.

  2. II

    Design

    Prototype the solution. Prove it holds on real data.

    We build a functional prototype on your stack — not a Figma. You evaluate against concrete criteria before a single line of production code ships.

  3. III

    Ship

    Engineer the product. Stay until it holds under load.

    Production engineering, evals, observability. We hand over running software with the paths to keep improving it — and we're around for the first release after launch.

05 What we believe

A short list. We keep to it.

  1. 01

    Ship, or it didn't happen.

    Strategy is an artifact of shipping. Nothing counts until it's live and measured.

  2. 02

    The model is not the product.

    Most of the work — evals, UX, failure modes, handoffs — is everything around the model.

  3. 03

    Specificity beats scale.

    A real problem, bounded tightly, beats a generic platform every time. We build for the domain.

  4. 04

    Fewer, sharper, owned.

    We run a small practice on purpose. One team per client. Senior on every call.
