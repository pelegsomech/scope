03 / Approach

Four things we believe.

Not a methodology. A set of operating beliefs that show up in everything we ship.

  1. 01

    Start with the business, not the model.

    Every engagement opens with the same question: what is the decision, cost, or revenue line this product is supposed to change? If we can't name it, we don't build it.

  2. 02

    Ship in weeks. Iterate in days.

    A working surface in front of real users beats a thousand-line spec every time. We get there fast, then sharpen on signal — not opinion.

  3. 03

    Fewer decks. More production code.

    Our deliverables look like shipped product — not Figma mockups and status reports. When we do write, we write to sharpen thinking, not to perform it.

  4. 04

    Own the outcome, not the hours.

    We agree on what "good" looks like at kickoff and work against it. No time-and-materials games, no scope creep taxes, no handoffs to a junior two weeks in.
