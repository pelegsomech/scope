Travel · Consumer App

Trip Spark.

AI-powered travel planning that unfolds.

The Friction

Travel planning is locked in spreadsheets. People spend hours copying addresses, cross-referencing maps, and organizing itineraries. It is a sterile, data-entry task masquerading as leisure. The friction is structural.

The Magic

We designed an "unfolding" user experience. Trip Spark automatically extracts EXIF data from photos to plot locations, dynamically updates real-time maps via Mapbox, and uses AI to logically sort and recommend itineraries. You don't build the map; the map builds itself around your intent.

The Result

A collaborative, state-managed canvas where plans feel fluid, not rigid. By integrating tight database subscriptions, travelers can collaboratively edit in real-time. We didn't make a better spreadsheet; we eliminated it.

Architecture & Stack
