Biotech · AI Research

Clinical Alpha.

Multi-agent intelligence for biotech investors.

The Friction

Biotech investing relies on massive, unstructured data: PDUFA dates, complex SEC filings, insider trading anomalies, and fragmented clinical trial data. Analysts spend days parsing PDFs to find the signal in the noise. The friction is cognitive overload.

The Magic

We didn't build a search tool; we built a team of digital analysts. Clinical Alpha employs an orchestrator that commands "squads" of specialized AI agents: the Short Squeeze Hunter, the SEC Filing Detective, the KOL Analyst.

The Result

Insights that took days to compile are surfaced in seconds. Through heavy parallelization on Firebase Functions, the agents scour the web, parse the SEC, and synthesize a single, actionable report. The interface is minimal, hiding the massive computational engine underneath.

Architecture & Stack

Vite & React 18 TypeScript Firebase Functions (Backend) Firestore Agentic Orchestration
